About Us

GoodGifts was created in 2026 by three students — Isabela, Emma, and Raphaël — as part of an entrepreneurship project. What started as a school assignment quickly became something we genuinely believed in.

We noticed a problem everyone faces: finding the right gift is hard, time-consuming, and often ends in a purchase that doesn't land. At the same time, the environmental cost of unwanted gifts — the packaging, the returns, the landfill — is enormous.

Our Vision

We believe gifting should be personal, effortless, and responsible. GoodGifts uses AI to ask smart, adaptive questions about the recipient and surface truly fitting gift ideas — each with a sustainability score, so you can feel good about what you're giving.

Our Values

  • Personalisation — every recommendation is tailored, never generic.
  • Sustainability — we prioritise eco-friendly products and flag their impact.
  • Simplicity — finding a great gift should take minutes, not hours.
  • Honesty — we show you the eco score of every product, even when it's low.

GoodGifts is currently in its early stage. We're a small team with a big ambition — to make every gift count.

How It Works

GoodGifts uses a smart, adaptive AI to find the perfect gift in three simple steps.

Step 1 — Tell us about the recipient

Start by describing the person you're buying for and the occasion. The more detail you give, the better — but even a few words is enough to get started. Our AI reads your input and decides how many follow-up questions (if any) are needed to narrow things down.

Step 2 — Answer a few smart questions (optional)

If our AI needs more information to give you great recommendations, it will ask a small number of targeted questions — adapted specifically to what you've already told us. There's no fixed list of 10 generic questions. You can skip any question or add your own answers.

Step 3 — Discover your gift picks

You'll receive up to 8 personalised gift ideas, each with a description, price range, category, and an eco-sustainability score from 0 to 100. Every gift links directly to Amazon so you can purchase in seconds.

What's an Eco Score?

Every gift is rated from 0 (environmentally harmful) to 100 (fully eco-friendly). Our algorithm considers factors like product durability, packaging, and the likelihood of the item being used long-term. We believe transparency matters — so even if a gift scores low, we still show you the score.

Why is this better than just Googling?

  • Search engines show you what's popular — we show you what's right for this person.
  • Amazon recommends based on sales — we recommend based on personality and occasion.
  • General AI tools aren't specialised for gifting — GoodGifts is built exclusively for this.
